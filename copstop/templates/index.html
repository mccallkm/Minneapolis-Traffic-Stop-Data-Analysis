Minneapolis Traffic Stop Analysis

Goal: This project will examine traffic citations of the Minneapolis, MN Police Department. Our analysis aims to identify correlations with gender and the number of citations issued in a given neighborhood.

  1. Determine if citations increased towards the end of the month
  2. Determine if citations vary by the day of the week. Do similar neighborhoods have similar patterns?
  3. Pinpoint what neighborhoods have the highest number of pull overs.
